Features

Features that make best for best

Manage Products

Manage Products means what you have available in the stock, and what you sold, and how much sold and how much purchased and also according to their respective companies.

Manage Customers

Manage customers records their details, when and how much they purchase and you sale to that and which products you sale to that customer, track recoveries transaction also fo customers.

Manage Transactions

Manage all transactions what sold and what purchase and how much pending transaction of the companies and how much recoveries pending of the customers and all ledgers.

Manage Order Bookers

Manage order bookers and their ledgers, means all track records of order booker how much sale they done and how much sale done of that product of company and how many recovery pendings.

Manage Sales

Manage sales of customers on their respective order bookers, where check and manage how much sales done till now and from date to date, how much sale have done of that customer or of that order booker.
